Welcome to a fan made Pokemon Tabletop United Character Creator.

Before we get started it is important to discuss a basic character concept with your game master. This system is dense with customization and a clear end goal can help you skim for abilities that work for you.

The first step is to choose which stats you're good at. Feel free to click each of the stats below to learn more about them.

  1. Pick a favorite and add 2 to it.
  2. Pick another stat to add 1 to.
  3. Choose 3 stats you feel your character will struggle with and subtract 1 from each.
