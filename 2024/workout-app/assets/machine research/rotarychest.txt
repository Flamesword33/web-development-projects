Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat and handle positions.
	3. Grasp handles.
	4. Extend and rotate arms forward as far as comfortable and return.
	5. If you can not bring hands together adjust handles closer to 6.
	   If you can not comfortably extend your arms adjust handles closer to 1.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return handles to their resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	Do not lean forward.
	Keep your back engaged.
