Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat position. Position leg bar just above your ankles. 
	3. Make sure that your knees are lined up with the red pivot.
	3. Grasp handles and place shins behind roller pad.
	4. Extend legs upward as far as comfortable and return.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return legs to resting position. 
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	As you exercise squeeze your quads.
	Do not let the weights hit as your legs return to start.
