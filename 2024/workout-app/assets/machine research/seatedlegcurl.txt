Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat and ankle pad position. Sit down and place ankles on roller pad.
	3. Sit with whole back against the seat. Make sure your knees line up with the red pivots.
	4. Lower thigh pad and grasp handles.
	5. Flex legs backward as far as comfortable and return.
	6. To exit machine - return weights to resting position then raise thigh pad.
	7. DO NOT attempt to exit machine with weights elevated.
	8. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	Put the bar just above your shoe.
	If too high use the flexibility knob on the bar.
