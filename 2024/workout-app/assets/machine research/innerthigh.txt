Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Position thigh pads against inside of leg above the knee.
	3. Lift adjustment disc handle and spread pads to desired setting.
	4. Release handle making sure the adjustment disk is engaged.
	5. Grasp hand grips. Support back with pad.
	6. Bring legs together then apart slowly, only go as far as comfortable.
	7. When done with the machine slowly move legs to resting position.
	8. Once weights are stationary lift adjustment disk handle and release tension on legs.
	6. Wipe down the machine before leaving.
