Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat and handle positions. Refer to adjustment guides.
	3. Grasp handles.
	4. Extend and rotate arms upward as far as comfortable and return.
	5. If you can not bring your hands together adjust handles closer to 6.
	   If you can not comfortably extend your arms, adjust handles closer to 1.
	6. When done slowly return handles to resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	Keep back pressed into the seat.
	Rotate handles forward from their resting position.
