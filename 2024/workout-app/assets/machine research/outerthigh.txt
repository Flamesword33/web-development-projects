Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Position thigh pads against outside of leg above knee.
	3. Lift adjustment disk handle and close pads to desired setting. 
	   Release handle making sure the adjustment disk is engaged.
	4. Grasp hand grips. Support back with pad.
	5. Move legs apart as far as comfortable and return together.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return weights and pads to resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.
