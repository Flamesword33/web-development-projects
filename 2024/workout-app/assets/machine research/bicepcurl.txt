Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat position.
	3. Grasp handles, placing arms on pads. Align elbows with red pivots.
	4. Ensure feet are flat on the floor, back is pressed flat against the seat.
	5. Flex arms upward as far as comfortable and return.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return handles to the ground.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.
