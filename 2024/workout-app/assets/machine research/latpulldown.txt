Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat position.
	3. Lower pad to thighs.
	4. Grasp handles and flex arms downward as far as comfortable and return.
	5. When done with the machine slowly return handles to their resting position.
	6. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	The Lat Pulldown machine works the same muscles as the Chin Dip machine.
	Keep feet flat on the floor.
	Keep back straight.
	Keep shoulders down and back.
