Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. If you are 5'5"(165 cm) or smaller use top tube.
	   If you are 5'6"(168 cm) or taller use the bottom tube.
	3. Position the balls of your feet on the non-stick pads.
	4. Grasp the handgrips and push back on the seat until your legs are
	   almost extended and you feel a stretch in your calves.
	5. Raise up on your toes lifting the weights. Return to beginning and repeat.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return seat and weights to their resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.
