Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Select seat position.
	3. Grasp handles, position elbows alongside torso.
	4. Ensure feet are flat on the floor, back is pressed flat against the seat.
	5. Flex arms downward until elbows are only slightly bent and return.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return handles to their resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	Refrain from using your legs or abdominal muscles.
