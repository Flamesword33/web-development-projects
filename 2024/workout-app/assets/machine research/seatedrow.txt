Instructions
	1. Always read and follow the instructions and warning labels on all machines.
	2. Sit down placing feet on platforms and keeping your knees slightly bent.
	3. Grasp handles and sit upright.
	4. While keeping your back straight, pull arms backward until elbows align with shoulders 
	   or as far as comfortable and return.
	6. When done with the machine slowly return handles to their resting position.
	7. Wipe down the machine before leaving.

Tips
	Refrain from using your back and abdominal muscles.
	Squeeze your shoulder blades as you preform the exercise.
